Our Story

We would have never guessed that what started as a casual friendship 5 years ago would turn into a happy relationship that keeps growing stronger to this day. It took a series of unlikely events and even a broken ankle for us to get together - but once we did, it felt like it was meant to be.

In the past 5 years we had a lot of fun together, traveled the world, picked up new hobbies and new languages - or at least we tried. We went through a whole year of lockdown in a small apartment and somehow we made it.

Now we are excited to take our relationship to the next level and we are inviting you to celebrate this special day with us!
